## A starter note for this trip

This page is a sample travel log so the structure is already in place before you replace it with your real writing.

The idea is simple: keep the text intimate and specific. Write about what the air felt like, what stayed in your ears after the ferry, what looked different after dark, or what tiny detail made the day yours.

You can keep each trip short. A few vivid paragraphs are often stronger than a long guide.

## Suggested structure

- Start with one scene that captures the trip immediately.
- Add two or three route moments that connect to the map on the right.
- End with one sentence that feels like the aftertaste of the trip.
